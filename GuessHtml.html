Guess Game

How to play the game

The computer is going to randomly select an integer from 1 to 100. You will keep guessing numbers until you find the computer's number, and the computer will tell you each time if your guess was too or too low. Trials are number of times you guess till you find computers number. The graph below will show you the trend of computer's selection, so that you can predict computer's next number to select.
